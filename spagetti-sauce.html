Grans Old Spaghetti Sauce

Description

This sweet spagetti sauce is easy to make and absolutely delicious. Originating from my grandmothers kitchen, my father taught me how to make a spagetti sauce that will smell so good it brings any mouth to water. This recipe will provide enough sauce for a family of 4-6 to enjoy for at least two dinners. You can always half the recipe to prevent any waste, or freeze extra sauce for a dinner next week!

Ingredients

  • 2 16oz cans of tomato Sauce
  • 1 canTomato Puree
  • Tomato Paste
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Garlic Powder
  • Garlic Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Sugar or Sugar Substitute
  • Oregeno

Steps

  1. Combine cans of tomato sauce and puree into large pot over low heat.
  2. Next, add sugar, italian seasoning, garlic and onion powder, and garlic salt. Fully incorporate seasoning and sugar and cover the pot with lid. Let simmer for at least 45minutes to 1 hour, cooking up to 6-8hours on low heat. Stir occasionally.
  3. After sauce has simmered for at least 45 minutes, add oregeno and tomato paste. Mix well, cook over low-medium heat while stirring occasionally, until tomato paste is fully incorporated, an additional 20+ minutes.
  4. Once your sauce is mixed well and the tomato paste has melted into the sauce, pour sauce over your choice of pasta noodles and enjoy!
