Hot Oatmeal

Description

If you like the sweet taste of Maple and Brown Sugar Oatmeal, then you will love this diabetic friendly oatmeal recipe!

Ingredients

  • Quaker Oats Oatmeal (I like to use the 1 minute or 5 minute oats)
  • 1 tsp - Artificial sweetner, splenda or something similar
  • 1 tsp - Artificial Brown Sugar (I use the Splenda brand)
  • 2 tsp - Cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp - Butter

Steps

  1. Follow directions on oatmeal package to cook the oats.
  2. After pouring the oatmeal into a bowl, add your other ingredients.
  3. Add a slice of buttered toast on the side, and enjoy!
